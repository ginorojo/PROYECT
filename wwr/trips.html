Friends Groups:

"Experience a thrilling adventure full of laughter as you conquer the rapids with your friends."

Group Package Options: We offer special packages for groups of friends, which include discounts based on the number of participants. To receive special discounts, click the "Contact Us" button, and we will call you to provide more information. This allows everyone to enjoy a quality rafting experience at an affordable price.

Advance Reservation Required: To ensure an optimal experience, reservations must be made at least 3 days in advance. This allows us to better organize groups and ensure there is enough equipment available for everyone.

Combine with paragliding

"Combine the best rafting with an incredible paragliding flight where you can feel like a true bird and enjoy unique views and sensations."

Combo Adventure: Experience the thrill of rafting combined with paragliding for an unforgettable adventure. This package allows you to enjoy both activities in one day, maximizing your fun and excitement.

Safety First: Our experienced guides prioritize safety during both rafting and paragliding. We provide all necessary equipment and instructions to ensure a secure and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Stunning Views: While paragliding, you'll have the opportunity to see breathtaking landscapes from above. Enjoy panoramic views of the river, mountains, and surrounding nature, making your adventure even more memorable.
