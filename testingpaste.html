History

Planet Rafting was founded in 2010 with a clear purpose: to offer unforgettable experiences on the most exciting rivers. From the start, we have been committed to combining the thrill of rafting with respect for nature, always ensuring safety and fun. Today, we are leaders in adventure tourism, taking our clients to explore breathtaking landscapes and live thrilling adventures.
